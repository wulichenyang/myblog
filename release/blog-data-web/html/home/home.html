Broad and Narrow Alley
A Nostalgic Area With An Old Face

Narrow alley is "slow life" area, shows the old chengdu compound culture, most of these compounds was dominated by has a style of bars, restaurants, tourist and wen-ching, firm in the alley, scattered throughout the afternoon, in time to stop. The narrow alley is a fine life style area with the theme of western catering, light dining, coffee, art leisure, healthy living and special cultural theme shop.The alley is close to the narrow lane, and the qing dynasty is called ruyi alley, or mingde hutong. Xinhai revolution renamed "well alley". In the remaining half of the street, a 500-meter long brick culture wall and a 500-meter long folk wall were built on the other side of the street.

Du Fu Thatched Cottage

Du fu thatched cottage museum in chengdu have repair the expansion in the past dynasties of song, yuan, Ming and qing, has evolved into a set of commemorative ancestral temple and the poet lives style pattern, construction of primitive simplicity and elegant, beautiful garden beautiful famous. The grass hall completely retains the layout of qing dynasty jiqing reconstruction, the total area of nearly 300 mu. The garden is a very unique "mixed" Chinese classical garden.
